Our Company

We are an innovative platform that connects pet owners with local pet service providers, including groomers, walkers, and trainers. Our user-friendly app allows pet owners to book services, read reviews, and manage their pet's health records all in one place. With a strong focus on community and safety. PetPal ensures that every service provider is vetted and rated by fellow pet lovers. We're on a mission to enhance the pet ownership experience and make it easier for pets to receie the care they deserve.
